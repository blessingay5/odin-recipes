Vegetable soup

About Vegetable soup (Efo riro)

Vegetable soup(Efo riro) is a rich and flavourful Nigeria dish, a native food of the weastern people (Yorubas), it is commonly made with spinach leave and known for it intense taste and great aroma, it is mostly served with swallow like Eba, Amala, Pounded yam, or even Rice.

It is an highly nutritious meal rich in iron and vitamins

Ingredients use in making Efo riro

  • spinach leaves (Efo shoko/Efo tete)
  • Pepper base (scotch bonnet, tomatoes, onion)
  • palm oil, locust beans (iru)
  • Assorted meat/fish

How to prepare vegetable soup

  1. Step 1: Prep: Firstly season the meats and boil until it is very tender, Secondly, soak the already sliced spinach in hot water.
  2. Step 2:Heat up palm oil, some onion,locust beans and pepper mix. Allow them to fry together properly
  3. Step 3:Add the cooked meat and stock fish to the sauce and allow to cook together
  4. Step 4:Add the spinach and allow it to cook for 3-5 minutes.
