Description

Puffpuff is a Nigeria snack, which features a deep fried, small size dough ball, usually golden brown, slightly crisp exterior and a soft, airy fluffy interior. It is a sweet and comfort snacks

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • sugar
  • salt
  • milk
  • Nutmeg(optional)

Steps to make PuffPuff

  1. Step 1: Add desired measurement of flour to a bowl, add yeast, grounded nutmeg, a pinch of salt to taste, granulated sugar, and stir properly making sure the dry ingredients combine well
  2. Step 2: Add warm water and mix properly for some time until the batter is smooth Note: Add enough water so you can easily scoop the batter with your palm.
  3. Step 3: Once properly combine, cover the bowl thightly allowing it to rise for about 30 minutes with the help of the yeast.
  4. Step 4: Heat up cooking oil and gently scoop the batter little by little into the hot oil. Allow this to fry until it is golden brown.
